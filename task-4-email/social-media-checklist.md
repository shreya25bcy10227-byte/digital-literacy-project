#  Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

##  Do’s
- Maintain a respectful and professional tone in all your posts and interactions  
- Verify the authenticity of information before sharing it online  
- Use privacy settings to protect your personal information  
- Share positive content such as achievements, skills, and learning experiences  
- Think carefully before posting to ensure it reflects your values and reputation  

##  Don’ts
- Do not share sensitive personal or financial information publicly  
- Avoid posting offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content  
- Do not engage in online arguments, trolling, or cyberbullying  
- Avoid spreading unverified news, rumors, or misinformation  
- Do not overshare details about your private life or daily activities  

---

##  Note
Responsible social media usage is essential for maintaining a positive digital footprint and ensuring safe and ethical online interactions.
