#  Stay Safe Online ¢ Prevention Checklist

##  General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for added security  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown attachments  
- Verify the authenticity of emails, messages, and websites  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for accessing sensitive information  
- Keep your devices and applications updated regularly  

##  Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown collect requests on UPI apps  
- Always verify the recipientÆs details before making payments
